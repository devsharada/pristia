Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common queries about product and services.

1. Do you sell real jewellery?

  • Yes, all our products are made with authentic materials. Each piece comes with quality assurance and certification (where applicable).

2. How can I place an order?

  • Simply browse our collection, add your favorite items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely using credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, or other available methods.

3. Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

  • No, We provide multiple payment options to make shopping easy for you.

4. What are your shipping charges?

  • Shipping is free on orders above [X amount]. For orders below that, a nominal shipping fee is applied at checkout.

5. How long will delivery take?

  • We usually dispatch within 2–4 business days. Delivery times depend on your location, typically between 5–10 business days.

6. Do you ship internationally?

  • Please contact our customer support for international shipping queries.

7. Can I return or exchange a product?

  • Yes. Products can be returned or exchanged within [7–14 days] of delivery, provided they are unused, in original packaging, and include all certificates/tags. Please see our Returns & Exchange Policy for details.

8. What if my jewellery arrives damaged?

  • In the rare case of damage during transit, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos/videos. We’ll arrange a replacement or refund.

9. Do you provide customization?

  • Yes, we accept customization requests on selected jewellery pieces. Contact our team to discuss your design preferences.

10. How do I care for my jewellery?

  • Store jewellery in a dry, soft pouch.
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, chemicals, or water.
  • Clean gently with a soft cloth to maintain shine.

11. Is my payment information secure?

  • Absolutely. We use encrypted payment gateways to ensure your personal and payment details remain safe.
