Return & Refund Policy

We value your trust and want you to love every purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied, our Return & Refund Policy makes the process simple.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Returns are accepted within [7–14 days] from the date of delivery.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards and vouchers.

3. Return Process

  • Contact our Customer Support at [Insert Email/Phone/WhatsApp] within the return window.

4. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be processed within [7–10 business days] after we receive and inspect the returned item.
